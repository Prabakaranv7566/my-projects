How to Configure Rules & Filters to Outlook

Process Steps

Creating rules and filters in Microsoft Outlook allows you to automate the organization of your emails by directing them to specific folders, marking them as read or unread, forwarding them, and more. Here's how you can create rules and filters in Outlook:

The steps might slightly vary depending on your Outlook version, but the basic process remains similar.

1.Open Outlook: Launch Microsoft Outlook and ensure you're logged in to your email account.

1 Right click to the mail which you want to give rules.Before that what are the options are available in filter you need to known.

  • From mail ID
  • Mail ID Contains
  • Subject
  • Subject Contains
  • Send to
  • Sent only to me
  • Marked as important
  • flagged as Action
  • Specific Word in message Header,Subject,Body of the mail
  • Automatic Reply
  • etc...
