If you are not sign up your Amazon program please follow this instruction

Sign up for the Amazon Associates program:
  • Go to the Amazon Associates website (affiliate-program.amazon.com).
  • Click the "Join Now” button.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to sign up for an Amazon Associates account. You will need an Amazon.com account if you don't have one already.

2.Log in to your Amazon Associates account:

  • Once you've signed up and your account is approved, log in to the Amazon Associates dashboard.

3.Create an Amazon affiliate link:

  • In your dashboard, click on "Product Linking" in the top menu, then select "Product Links" from the drop-down menu.
  • Enter the product you want to promote in the search bar. You can search for a specific product or browse Amazon's product categories.
  • Select the product you want to promote from the search results.
  • Customize your affiliate link settings, including the link type (text and image, text only, image only), and associate tags if you have multiple tracking IDs.
  • Click on the "Get HTML" button.

4.Generate the HTML code for the banner:

  • In the "Get HTML" pop-up, you'll see a preview of the product link. Below the preview, you can choose the banner size and style. Amazon offers various banner options to fit different website layouts.
  • Select the banner size and style that suits your needs.

5.Copy the HTML code:

  • After selecting your banner preferences, click on the "Highlight HTML" button to select the HTML code, then copy it to your clipboard (Ctrl+C on Windows/Linux or Command+C on macOS).

6.Add the banner to your website:

  • Log in to your website's content management system (CMS) or wherever you manage your website.
  • Create a new post or page where you want to display the Amazon affiliate banner.
  • Paste the HTML code you copied from the Amazon Associates dashboard into the appropriate section of your post or page. You may need to use the HTML or custom code editor, depending on your CMS.

6.Preview and publish:

  • Preview your post or page to ensure that the Amazon affiliate banner displays correctly.
  • You are ready to publish your post.
